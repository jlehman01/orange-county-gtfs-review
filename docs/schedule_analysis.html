The tables and charts presented on this page provide a look at the scheduling information contained in each GTFS feed. These tables are intended to assist an analysis in understanding service patterns to ensure that they are accurate. This information is also helpful in ensuring that overlapping schedules are not computed, resulting in duplicate service findings.
